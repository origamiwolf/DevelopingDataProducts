Distance Estimator

Using the linear models obtained from the SMC Cepheid period-magnitude observations as a distance calibration, the distance to Cepheids in other regions of space, eg other galaxies, can be estimated from their observed periods and apparent magnitudes.

To estimate the distance, at least two observable attributes of the Cepheid are required:
  • The light curve period of the Cepheid
  • Either the maximum apparent magnitude of the Cepheid, and/or
  • the minimum apparent magnitude of the Cepheid
