This table shows the 25 stars identified by Leavitt in 1912 as being variable, and whose periods correlate with their magnitudes.

The columns are:
  • H: Harvard number of the star
  • Epoch: Epoch in days
  • Period: Period in days
  • magVal: Maximum or minimum magnitude
  • appMag: Apparent magnitude of star
  • absMag: Absolute magnitude of star

The first 5 columns are obtained from Leavitt (1912). The last column is computed from the apparent magnitude of the star and the estimated distance to the Small Magellanic Cloud.
