This table shows the 25 stars identified by Leavitt in 1912 as being variable, and whose periods correlate with their magnitudes. Data is sourced from Reference 2.

The columns are:
  • H: Harvard number of the star
  • Epoch: Epoch in days
  • Period: Period in days
  • magVal: Maximum or minimum magnitude
  • appMag: Apparent magnitude of star
